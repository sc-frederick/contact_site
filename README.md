# Stephen Frederick - Digital Business Card

A modern, responsive digital business card for Stephen Frederick, Staff Engineer at Advanced Engineering Consultants.

## Overview

This project serves as a personal digital business card, providing a clean and professional way to share contact information and professional services online.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Modern UI**: Clean card-based layout with subtle animations and gradients
- **Contact Integration**: 
  - Click to call
  - Click to email
  - Google Maps integration for location
  - Save contact as vCard
  - Share profile functionality
- **Service Showcase**: Visual display of engineering and development services offered
